Help & Support

Find guidance for attendance enrollment, login, and camera support.

Last updated: May 2026

Getting Started

  • Register as a student or lecturer from the Portals page.
  • Enroll your face using the camera on the Student portal.
  • Join an active attendance session using the session token provided by your lecturer.

Facing Camera Issues?

  • Use a modern browser such as Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.
  • Open the app at http://localhost:5000 or use HTTPS for secure camera access.
  • Allow the browser to access your webcam when prompted.

Login Problems

  • Check that the email and password are correct.
  • Lecturers must use the lecturer login page; students use the student login page.
  • If your account is locked, restart the browser and try again.

Need More Help?

Our support team is available to help with registration, camera setup, and attendance record questions.
