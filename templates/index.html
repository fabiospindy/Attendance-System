How It Works

Simple, secure, and automated attendance in three steps

1. Lecturer Creates Session

Lecturer logs in, generates a unique attendance session link, and shares it with students for the class.

2. Students Enroll Face

First-time students register by capturing their face data. This one-time enrollment enables future automatic recognition.

3. Auto Attendance

Students use the session link, and their attendance is marked instantly via AI-powered face recognition.
